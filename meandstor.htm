    Summer in NYUMVB: Junior libero David Ho is busy spending his summer creating a new iPhone app called Stor. Stor (@stor.ios ) is a peer to peer self-storage application that connects space to users who have a demand for that space. Unlike brick and mortar storage companies, Stor offers better prices in addition to more convenience and flexibility. After going through an arduous process of storing items over the summer, David was motivated to create a platform that modernizes the storage industry. The app will be releasing in spring of 2019 so everyone look out and stay tuned!
    .
